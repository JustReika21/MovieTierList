About MovieTierList

MovieTierList is a platform where everyone can share reviews about movies, serials, games, books, and much more. Our goal is to create a space where opinions are valued and accessible to everyone.
Stack: Python, Django, Django Rest Framework, PostgreSQL, pytest, swagger, drf-spectacular, Docker, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
github: MovieTierList

Our Mission

To make reviews simple, honest, and available for all kinds of content — from blockbuster films to indie projects and everything in between.
